This is an interpretation of the classic game scrabble to be played single player. It plays a bit diffrently from how regular scrabble plays, so listen up to the following rules:

  • The goal is to get the highest score
  • Word score is calculated by value of tiles in play and bonuses
  • You can discard tiles in your hand, but that will subtract their value from the total score
  • You can always resart the game or reset the current word, but discarded tiles will never come back
  • The bag holds the tiles that are remaining but not in play, also known as "Tiles left"
  • Once you have no moves left, you can select "End Game"
  • If you end the game with extra tiles in your bag, the total value of those tiles will be subtracted from your score, so use as many as you can!
  • Your total score is calculated by the following: score = word values - discarded tiles - remaining tiles in the bag

Good Luck!
